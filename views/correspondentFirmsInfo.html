Correspondent Firms Information

Correspondent firms are not full member firms of Moore Stephens International Limited.

Firms may be designated as correspondent or associated firms where:

  • the firm does not represent Moore Stephens International Limited exclusively; or
  • the firm does not wish to comply with one or more of the other administrative criteria necessary for full membership; or
  • the firm is in a development phase to seeking full membership of Moore Stephens International Limited.
